Facilities

All our facilities are open 24 hours a day for the convenience of our patrons.

  • Licensed Bar

    • We have a fully functional bar which is open 24x7 with live music for our patrons to unwind and relax. Happy hour everyday from 6-7pm.

    We are sorry that patrons who look below 21 would not be served alcoholic drinks without a valid ID. Inconvenience is regretted.

  • Volleyball Court

    • We have multiple courts to accomodate more teams to play at the same time. We have both indoor and outdoor courts to cater to the needs of our patrons and climate conditions. Courts can be booked at any time except when a Tournament is going on.

  • Changing and Locker Room

    • Adequate changing rooms and locker rooms are provided for men and women. All our changing rooms have shower and toilet facilities with free lockers available in ample supply.

  • Beach

    • We have a private beach for the enjoyment of our patrons. Our beaches are equipped with beach chairs, towels and other beach equipment. It is the perfect for a game of frizbee or informal volleyball. Two of our three beaches can also be booked for private functions.
