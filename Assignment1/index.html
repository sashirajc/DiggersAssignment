Welcome to Digger's beach volleyball.

Digger's is the perfect place to have fun while you become fit. You can enjoy our bar that is open all night and play at the beach under the watch of our life guards any time of the day or night. Join us and learn a new sport from our expert volleyball coaches.Volleyball does not require extreme fitness. It can be a low-impact sport for those who want to take it slow and it can also be fast for those who like the adrenalin. Take part in tournaments and become the champion of Marakson!

Come hang out at Digger's beach volleyball if you are looking for a place to have fun. To know more about volleyball Click the ball and get started!!
