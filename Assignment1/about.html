About Us

Digger's beach Volleyball was established in January 2007 in Marakson to meet the growing interest from families for more opportunities to play volleyball in a structured and competitive environment.

Our programs are well structured to meet the needs of boys and girls, whether beginner, intermediate or advanced players. To date, we have received over 300 player registrations for our club programs and camps.

At Digger's, we believe that the gaming experience is complete only when we cater to the needs of those who look to volleyball as a form of recreation and relaxation just as well as we cater to those interested in competitve sport.

We are committed to be the leading provider of high quality volleyball instruction and competition for the youth in the Marakson area:

  • Our teams are led by qualified, well paid full time coaches who are available 24 hours a day
  • Our Coach:Player ratios are maintained at 1:12 or better. We utilize top-notch facilities and equipment
  • Our facilities are open 24 hours a day with staff available round the clock.

Drop by our club to experience what quality volleyball feels like!
