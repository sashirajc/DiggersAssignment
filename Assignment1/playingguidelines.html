Playing guidelines

While playing at our facilities we would like you to keep these in mind.

  • Players at Digger's play at their own risk and responsible for their own safety. We are not responsible for lost property or any other accidents that may happen at our facilities.
  • Players must involve in sports only if they are physically fit. Please check with your doctors if you are not sure.
  • Game fees must be paid prior to the start of the game
  • Games may be cancelled 4 hours before the commencement of the game to qualify for a full refund.
  • Games cancelled less than 4 hours before commencement shall forefeit the game and lost upto 50% of the deposit.
